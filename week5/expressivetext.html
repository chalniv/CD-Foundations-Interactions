Where have I been?

Listen, I have been

diffused by clouds.

diffused by clouds.

You can see me dancing in the foam on the crest of ocean waves. I rise into the air in a fleeting moment of euphoric suspension.

Where have I been?

Listen, I have been

diffused by clouds.

diffused by clouds.

And you can hear me as I crash back, break the surface, and sink into the luminescent blue bubbles.

Where have I been?

Look, you can see me as I linger on the dark sand beaches at night. I glow across the horizon line you set your foot on reaching in both directions for miles.

Where have I been?

Listen, I have been

diffused by clouds.

diffused by clouds.

I have joined the moon, I am now her light. You can see me settle upon your nose and cheeks, upon the trees, upon the fingers you run along the seams of your childhood bed sheets.
